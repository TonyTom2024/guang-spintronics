Research

Neuromorphic Computing via Spintronics

We leverage voltage-tunable spin-torque ferromagnetic resonance (ST-FMR) in artificial skyrmion lattices to develop highly efficient hardware for neuromorphic computing.

基于自旋电子学的神经形态计算：利用人工斯格明子晶格中的电压可调自旋矩铁磁共振现象，开发高能效的类脑计算硬件。
[ Research Image Placeholder 1 ]

Topological Spin Textures & Dynamics

Focusing on the creation, manipulation, and high-frequency dynamics of non-trivial spin textures, including skyrmions and antiskyrmions driven by electrical currents.

拓扑自旋结构与动力学：致力于探索电流驱动下的斯格明子、反斯格明子等非平庸自旋结构的生成、调控及高频动力学行为。
[ Research Image Placeholder 2 ]

Advanced Micromagnetic Simulation

Integrating Mumax3 simulations with precision measurements (Keithley switch matrices & SRS lock-in amplifiers) to uncover the underlying mechanisms of emergent topological states.

先进微磁模拟与精密测量：结合 Mumax3 模拟与高精度仪器测量，揭示自旋演生的底层物理机制。
