School of Materials, Ningbo Eastern Institute of Technology

Welcome to Our Group

We investigate topological magnetism, novel spin textures, and their integration into next-generation spintronic devices. By combining advanced micromagnetic simulations with precision laboratory measurements, we explore emergent states towards energy-efficient neuromorphic computing.

Current Research Themes

  • Neuromorphic Computing: Utilizing spintronic devices for hardware-based artificial intelligence.
  • Artificial Skyrmion Lattices: Voltage-tunable spin-torque resonance and dynamics.
  • Topological Spin Textures: Confined antiskyrmion motion driven by electric current excitations.
  • Advanced Simulations & Measurements: Multiscale modeling (Mumax3) alongside precision lock-in amplifier and switch matrix techniques.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Guang Yao

Dr. Yao received his Ph.D. in condensed matter physics in June 2021. Prior to establishing his independent research group at the Ningbo Eastern Institute of Technology in 2026, he accumulated extensive research experience as a postdoctoral fellow at RIKEN (Japan), CNRS (France), and within Saudi Arabia.
